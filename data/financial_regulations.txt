The Reserve Bank of India regulates digital lending platforms to prevent predatory practices and ensure borrower protection.

Financial institutions must follow KYC (Know Your Customer) guidelines to prevent fraud and money laundering.

High leverage in financial portfolios increases systemic risk and may lead to instability during market volatility.

Non-performing assets (NPAs) affect the financial health of banks and require strict monitoring by regulators.

Investment portfolios with excessive exposure to a single asset class may lead to higher financial risk.

Diversification is a key strategy for reducing financial risk in investment portfolios.
